## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy explains what you may not do when using OneAIHub. It applies to all prompts, uploads, reference materials, generated results, account activity, and any attempts to use OneAIHub services or infrastructure.

## Prohibited Content

You may not use OneAIHub to create, request, upload, transform, or distribute:

- NSFW, sexually explicit, sexually suggestive, fetish, or pornographic content.
- Any sexualized content involving minors, age ambiguity, school-age depictions, or exploitative scenarios.
- Deepfake, face-swap, face-manipulation, impersonation, or deceptive identity content.
- Content that violates privacy, publicity, copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights.
- Illegal, abusive, harassing, hateful, violent, extremist, or fraudulent content.
- Malware, phishing material, spam, or content intended to harm systems, users, or third parties.

## Prohibited Conduct

You may not:

- Attempt to bypass safety systems, moderation checks, payment controls, or usage limits.
- Use OneAIHub to mislead others about the origin or authenticity of generated content.
- Resell, scrape, reverse engineer, or misuse OneAIHub without authorization.
- Use another person’s identity, likeness, or private material without the rights needed to do so.

## Enforcement

OneAIHub screens image and video prompts before generation. We may block requests, suspend access, cancel subscriptions, remove content, or retain records needed for legal, fraud, safety, or abuse investigations.

## Support

If you have questions about this policy, contact **support@oneaihub.online**.
