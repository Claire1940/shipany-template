## Introduction

Welcome to OneAIHub, an all-in-one AI platform for AI chat, AI image generation, AI music generation, and creative AI tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our website and services at [https://www.oneaihub.online](https://www.oneaihub.online).

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to operate and improve OneAIHub:

1. **Account Information**
   - Name, email address, login credentials, authentication records, and account preferences.
   - Used to create your account, sign you in, provide customer support, and secure your account.

2. **Usage and AI Activity Data**
   - AI chat prompts, AI chat conversation history, AI image generation tasks, AI music generation tasks, uploaded images or reference materials, generated results, feature usage, and task status.
   - Used to provide AI features, maintain service history, troubleshoot issues, improve product quality, and prevent abuse.

3. **Credits, Orders, and Payment Records**
   - Credit balance changes, credit usage records, subscription or purchase records, order identifiers, billing status, and payment provider transaction references.
   - Used to process payments, apply credits, provide invoices or receipts, prevent fraud, and resolve billing disputes.

4. **Device, Log, and Analytics Data**
   - IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, pages visited, timestamps, error logs, and analytics events.
   - Used to keep the service reliable, understand product usage, detect abuse, and improve performance.

5. **Cookies and Similar Technologies**
   - Cookies or local storage may be used for authentication, preferences, analytics, and security.
   - You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain AI chat, AI image generation, AI music generation, credits, payments, and user account features.
- Authenticate users and protect accounts from unauthorized access.
- Process orders, subscriptions, credits, refunds, and customer support requests.
- Monitor service performance, troubleshoot errors, and improve OneAIHub.
- Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, policy violations, or security incidents.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

## Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary:

- With service providers that help us operate hosting, authentication, payments, analytics, email, AI generation, storage, and customer support.
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal process, or government requests.
- To protect the rights, safety, property, or security of OneAIHub, our users, or others.
- In connection with a business transfer such as merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets.

## Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including access controls and security monitoring. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

AI prompts, uploaded materials, and generated outputs may be processed by third-party AI infrastructure providers solely for providing the requested AI functionality, subject to their applicable terms and privacy practices.

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent abuse, and maintain business records. You may contact us to request deletion of your account or certain personal information, subject to legal, security, and operational requirements.
