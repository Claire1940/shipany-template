OneAIHub is an all-in-one AI workspace for people who want to create text, images, and music without switching between separate tools.

## What you can do with OneAIHub

OneAIHub focuses on three core creative workflows:

- **AI Chat**: brainstorm ideas, draft content, summarize information, and ask follow-up questions.
- **AI Image Generator**: turn prompts into images for creative projects, marketing assets, and visual experiments.
- **AI Music Generator**: create songs, instrumentals, melodies, and background tracks from text prompts.

## How credits work

OneAIHub uses credits to keep usage clear and predictable. You can start with free trial credits, then choose a one-time credit pack or a recurring plan from the [Pricing](/pricing) page.

Typical usage:

| Feature | Example credit use |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| AI Chat | Lightweight text conversations |
| AI Image Generator | Prompt-based image creation |
| AI Music Generator | Text-to-music generation tasks |

## Account history

Your account keeps records for:

- payment orders
- credit grants and usage
- AI generation tasks
- chat history

This makes it easier to review what you created and manage your remaining credits.

## Start creating

Open [AI Chat](/chat), [AI Image Generator](/ai-image-generator), or [AI Music Generator](/ai-music-generator) to begin. If you need more credits, visit [Pricing](/pricing).
